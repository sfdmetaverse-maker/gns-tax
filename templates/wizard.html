Next Steps: Filing with CRA

  • 1
    File T2 Return
    Use CRA My Business Account or certified software (e.g. TurboTax T2, Wealthsimple). Due 6 months after fiscal year-end.
  • 2
    Pay Corporate Tax
    Due 2 months after year-end (3 months for small CCPCs). Pay via CRA My Payment or your bank.
  • 3
    File GST/HST Return
    Via CRA My Business Account or GST/HST NETFILE. Annual filers: 3 months after year-end.
  • 4
    Keep Records
    Store all receipts, bank statements, and this summary for 6 years in case of audit.

This tool provides estimates for preparation purposes. Consult an accountant for complex situations. All data is stored locally on your computer.
