## Top Stories

### 1. OpenAI Shuts Down Sora — Disney Deal Dead
OpenAI killed its AI video app Sora after burning ~$15M/day in compute with only $2.1M in lifetime revenue. Downloads had plunged 75% from their November peak. Disney walked away from a planned $1B investment. OpenAI is redirecting resources toward AGI and a new model codenamed **"Spud."**
- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/24/openais-sora-was-the-creepiest-app-on-your-phone-now-its-shutting-down/)
- [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/openai-is-shutting-down-sora-its-powerful-ai-video-app)

### 2. Anthropic vs. Pentagon — AI Goes to War
The DOD designated Anthropic a "supply-chain risk" after the company refused to allow Claude for mass surveillance or autonomous weapons. Over 30 employees from OpenAI and Google DeepMind (including Jeff Dean) filed a brief supporting Anthropic. This case is reshaping the legal framework for military AI use.
- [MIT Technology Review](https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/03/25/1134571/the-ai-hype-index-ai-goes-to-war/)
- [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/09/openai-and-google-employees-rush-to-anthropics-defense-in-dod-lawsuit/)

### 3. White House Releases National AI Policy Framework
The biggest U.S. AI policy development — calls for federal preemption of state AI laws, routes oversight through existing agencies (FTC, FCC, SEC), explicitly rejects creating a new federal AI body. Includes child safety requirements and declares training on copyrighted material is not a violation.
- [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/trump-ai-policy-framework.html)
- [Nextgov](https://www.nextgov.com/artificial-intelligence/2026/03/white-house-releases-regulatory-vision-ai/412274/)

### 4. OpenClaw Goes Viral — Jensen Calls It "The Next ChatGPT"
Open-source AI agent framework by Peter Steinberger (now at OpenAI) wraps Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Grok, letting users interact via iMessage, Discord, Slack, or WhatsApp. Became the most-starred GitHub project ever. China's tech giants are holding public events encouraging mass adoption.
- [a16z Top 100 Gen AI Apps](https://a16z.com/100-gen-ai-apps-6/)

### 5. Claude Opus 4.6 Solves Open Math Problem
Donald Knuth published a paper titled "Claude's Cycles" after Claude Opus 4.6 solved an open graph theory problem he'd been working on for weeks. Knuth called it a "dramatic advance in automatic deduction and creative problem solving."
- [Radical Data Science](https://radicaldatascience.wordpress.com/2026/03/20/ai-news-briefs-bulletin-board-for-march-2026/)

### 6. OpenAI Building Fully Automated AI Researcher
OpenAI is pouring resources into an autonomous agent-based AI researcher — an "autonomous AI intern" by September 2026, then a fully automated multi-agent research system by 2028. Now OpenAI's stated North Star.
- [MIT Technology Review](https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/03/20/1134438/openai-is-throwing-everything-into-building-a-fully-automated-researcher/)

### 7. Meta Cuts 700 Jobs, Pivots Further Into AI
Meta eliminated ~700 roles (Reality Labs, recruiting, sales, Facebook) to refocus on AI. Simultaneously unveiled exec stock packages worth up to $921M each over five years to retain AI leadership.
- [Tech Startups](https://techstartups.com/2026/03/26/top-tech-news-today-march-26-2026/)
